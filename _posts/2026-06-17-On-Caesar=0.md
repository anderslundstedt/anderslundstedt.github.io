At The Swedish Congress of Philosophy 2026, I gave a presentation based on joint
work with [Eric Johannesson](https://ericjohannesson.com/). We have done some
thinking inspired by Frege's “Caesar problem”—the “problem” that Frege's
logicist project “failed” to rule out the identification of Ceasar with the
number 0.
